Disease Information

  • Influenza

    Influenza - Disease Details

    Influenza (Flu) is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. It can cause mild to severe illness and can lead to hospitalization or death.

  • Dengue Fever

    Dengue Fever - Disease Details

    Dengue Fever is a viral illness transmitted by mosquitoes. Symptoms include high fever, headache, pain behind the eyes, joint and muscle pain, and rash.

  • Pneumonia

    Pneumonia - Disease Details

    Pneumonia is an infection of the lungs that causes inflammation and fluid in the air sacs, leading to difficulty breathing and fever.

  • Typhoid

    Typhoid - Disease Details

    Typhoid is a bacterial infection caused by Salmonella typhi, leading to symptoms such as fever, abdominal pain, and weakness.

  • Jaundice

    Jaundice - Disease Details

    Jaundice is a condition where the skin and eyes turn yellow due to excess bilirubin. It is often a sign of liver problems such as hepatitis.

  • Eczema

    Eczema - Disease Details

    Eczema is a condition that causes the skin to become red, itchy, and inflamed. It can occur due to allergic reactions or environmental factors.

  • Chickenpox

    Chickenpox - Disease Details

    Chickenpox is a highly contagious viral infection that causes itchy rashes, fever, and fatigue.

  • Tuberculosis

    Tuberculosis - Disease Details

    Tuberculosis (TB) is a bacterial infection that primarily affects the lungs, causing coughing, weight loss, and fatigue.

  • Asthma

    Asthma - Disease Details

    Asthma is a chronic disease that causes difficulty breathing due to inflammation and narrowing of the airways. Symptoms include wheezing and shortness of breath.

  • COVID-19

    COVID-19 - Disease Details

    COVID-19 is a viral respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It leads to symptoms such as fever, cough, and loss of taste or smell.

  • Hypertension

    Hypertension - Disease Details

    Hypertension (high blood pressure) is a condition where the blood pressure in the arteries is consistently high, increasing the risk of heart disease and stroke.

  • Polio

    Polio - Disease Details

    Polio is a viral infection that can cause paralysis and is spread through contaminated water or food.

  • Malaria

    Malaria - Disease Details

    Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by parasites transmitted through the bites of infected mosquitoes, causing fever, chills, and flu-like symptoms.

  • Hepatitis

    Hepatitis - Disease Details

    Hepatitis is an inflammation of the liver, often caused by viral infections. Hepatitis A, B, and C are the most common types.

  • Migraine

    Migraine - Disease Details

    Migraines are severe headaches often accompanied by nausea, vomiting, and sensitivity to light and sound.

  • Cholera

    Cholera - Disease Details

    Cholera is an infectious disease caused by the bacterium Vibrio cholerae, leading to severe diarrhea and dehydration.

  • Measles

    Measles - Disease Details

    Measles is a highly contagious viral disease that causes a rash, fever, and coughing. It can lead to serious complications.

  • Anemia

    Anemia - Disease Details

    Anemia is a condition where there is a lack of healthy red blood cells, leading to fatigue, weakness, and pale skin.
